# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple professional standard:

- be respectful
- be direct
- focus on technical substance
- assume good intent
- avoid harassment, hostility, and personal attacks

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- abusive or discriminatory language
- intimidation or threats
- bad-faith disruption
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate these standards.
